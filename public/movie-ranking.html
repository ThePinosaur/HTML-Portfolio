Claudia's Favourite Movies of All Time

My top 5 movies in no particular order

Spirited Away

This is a beautifully animated movie with complex and interesting female characters, including the lead.

Life is Beautiful

This movie is not just a WWII movie, it is a movie about a parent's love and devotion to their child even in the hardest of times.

Howl's Moving Castle

Another beautiful movie by Hayao Miyazaki with another strong female lead. This movies could be considered as a fantasy romance film but it is so much more than that because of the rich animation and complexity in characters.

Pan's Labyrinth

Another movie set during war, the spanish civil war, with another compelx female lead. This story is about a little girl who is trying to prove she belongs in the fantasy world away from reality. Lots of amazing special effects and a captivating story.

Guillermo del Toro's Pinocchio

I just realized most of my favourite movies are set during war times but do not focus on war. This is the story of pinocchio told by the amazing Guillermo del Toro, which means you will find amazing visuals and a complex and hearfelt story about a parent and their child.
